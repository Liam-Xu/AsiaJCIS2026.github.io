Notice for Authors of Accepted Papers
  • For each accepted paper, at least one of the authors is required to register as a General Participant (Offline Attendance).
  • This applies regardless of whether the registering author is a student or not.
  • In cases where both a student and a non-student author attend, the student may register as Student, while the non-student author must register as General Participant.

Registration Fees (USD)

Category Fee
Student Offline Attendance 400 USD
General Participant Offline Attendance 500 USD

Registration includes: offline conference participation, access to online proceedings, two lunches, one banquet, and a souvenir.

Complete your registration payment here

Register & Pay Online

On-site registration is also available at the venue (credit card only, same fees apply).

Step 2 — Submit Your Paper Registration Form

After completing payment, you must submit the paper registration form.

Once your payment is confirmed, please fill in and submit the paper registration form with the following information:

1
Paper Identification
Paper ID

Enter the Paper ID as assigned in the EasyChair submission system.

2
Paper Details
Paper Title Author Name(s)

Provide the full title of the paper and the names of all authors exactly as they appear in the submission.

3
Registrant Information
Your Name

Provide the name of the person completing the registration (the attending presenter).

Fields marked * are required. Incomplete submissions may result in processing delays. Please contact asiajcis2026@easychair.org if you need assistance.

注册及报告者截止日期： 待定，2026年 (UTC+08:00)
每篇录用论文或海报至少需有一位作者在截止日期前完成注册。
录用论文作者须知
  • 每篇录用论文至少需要一位作者以普通参会者（线下出席）身份完成注册。
  • 无论注册者是否为学生，上述要求均适用。
  • 若论文中同时有学生作者和非学生作者参会，学生作者可以学生身份注册，而非学生作者须以普通参会者身份注册。

注册费用（人民币 CNY）

类别 费用
学生 线下出席 2,800 CNY
普通参会者 线下出席 3,500 CNY

注册费包含：线下会议参与资格、在线论文集访问权限、两次午餐、一次晚宴及纪念品。

点击下方链接完成注册缴费

中国参会者注册缴费入口

第二步 — 提交论文注册表

完成缴费后，您需要提交论文注册表。

缴费确认后，请填写并提交论文注册表，需包含以下信息：

1
论文编号
Paper ID（论文编号）

请填写 EasyChair 投稿系统中分配的论文编号。

2
论文信息
论文题目（Paper Title） 作者姓名（Author Name(s)）

请提供与投稿系统中完全一致的论文题目及全部作者姓名。

3
注册人信息
您的姓名（Your Name）

请填写完成注册的参会者本人姓名（即出席报告的作者）。

标有 * 的字段为必填项。信息不完整可能导致处理延迟。如需帮助，请联系 asiajcis2026@easychair.org。
