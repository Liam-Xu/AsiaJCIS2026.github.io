Overview

We are pleased to announce that the 21st Asia Joint Conference on Information Security (AsiaJCIS 2026) opens a call for POSTERS focused on topics relevant to the conference. You can present ongoing studies and research projects.

The poster session will be part of the conference program and if accepted, at least one author must present the poster during the poster session.

Poster Specifications
  • Posters should clearly explain the contents of your study. Diagrams are recommended over text-heavy descriptions.
  • Poster size: A0 — 118.9 cm (H) × 84.1 cm (W)  /  46.8 in (H) × 33.1 in (W)
  • May consist of a single printed poster or separate pieces.
  • Poster presenters must bring their own poster to the conference venue.
  • Poster boards and pushpins will be provided by the conference.
  • Submissions and presentations must be in English.

Publication Policy

Poster presentations are not rigorously refereed, but posters out of the scope of the conference may be turned down by the poster committee.

The copyrights of any content of the poster presentations are retained by the authors. Presenting a poster at AsiaJCIS 2026 does not prevent its submission to other journals or conferences.

Instructions for Authors

To present a poster, authors must email the following information to:

asiajcis2026@easychair.org
  • Title of the poster
  • Author(s) names and affiliations
  • Abstract — one or two paragraph summary
  • Name of person presenting the poster

At least one author is required to register for the conference and present the poster. Please register as early as possible.
